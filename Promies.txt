What is a callback?
  function you call into another function.

Callback hell
  -functions within functions within functions.
  -many layers.

How to solve Callback Hell?
  -Promises!
    -Pending(not fulfilled or rejected)
    -Fulfilled(completed)
    -Rejected(error)
